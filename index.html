off the grid*
The alternative social media landscape of 2021
*as stated by "MeWe"

In this website you will find a catalogue of alternative social media and filters to explore the main themes related to them.

Words are the core of the project, self descriptions of Social Media guided the research. Alternative Social Media are categorised based on their payoffs.

The emerging themes extracted from payoffs provide a photography of the current social cultural environment.
